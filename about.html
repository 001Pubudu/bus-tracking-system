🎯 Our Mission

At BusTrack, we believe public transport should be transparent, reliable, and stress-free for everyone. We built this platform to bridge the information gap between bus operators and passengers — delivering real-time GPS data, smart alerts, and easy-to-read schedules so you always know where your bus is.

Whether you're a daily commuter, a fleet manager, or a city planner, BusTrack gives you the visibility you need to make smarter decisions and keep people moving.

How BusTrack Works

A simple pipeline from GPS device to your screen.

📡

1. GPS Collection

Each bus transmits its GPS coordinates every 10 seconds via an on-board device.

☁️

2. Cloud Processing

Our servers process location data and calculate accurate ETAs in real-time.

🗺️

3. Live Map Update

The map refreshes automatically — passengers see up-to-the-second positions.

🔔

4. Smart Alerts

Delays, arrivals, and route changes are pushed to users instantly via notifications.
